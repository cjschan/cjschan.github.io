Quiz 10

Exponential and Logarithmic Functions

Worked Solutions

Covers Sections 5.3, 5.4

Instructions. This is a take-home quiz. You may use your lecture notes and online resources, but the work you submit must be your own. Show your work clearly to receive credit.

Total: 10 points

Problem 1 [2 pts]

Evaluate without a calculator. (1 pt each)

  1. \(\log_2 64\)
  2. \(\log_5 \dfrac{1}{125}\)

(a) \(\log_2 64 = 6\) (since \(2^6 = 64\)).

(b) \(\log_5 \tfrac{1}{125} = -3\).

Problem 2 [2 pts]

Combine as a single logarithm: \(3 \ln x + \tfrac{1}{2} \ln y - 2 \ln z\).

\(\ln \dfrac{x^3 \sqrt{y}}{z^2}\).

Problem 3 [2 pts]

An investment of \$2,500 grows at 5% annual interest, compounded continuously. How long until it doubles? Round to two decimals.

\(2P = P e^{0.05 t}\), so \(\ln 2 = 0.05 t\), \(t = \dfrac{\ln 2}{0.05} \approx 13.86\) years.

Problem 4 [2 pts]

Solve \(2 e^{3x} = 30\). Round to three decimals.

Divide: \(e^{3x} = 15\). Take \(\ln\): \(3x = \ln 15 \approx 2.708\), so \(x \approx 0.903\).

Problem 5 [2 pts]

Solve \(\log (x + 1) + \log (x - 2) = 1\). Watch for extraneous solutions.

Combine: \(\log[(x + 1)(x - 2)] = 1\), so \((x + 1)(x - 2) = 10\). Expand: \(x^2 - x - 12 = 0\), factor \((x - 4)(x + 3) = 0\). Domain requires \(x > 2\), so reject \(-3\). Solution: \(x = 4\).
